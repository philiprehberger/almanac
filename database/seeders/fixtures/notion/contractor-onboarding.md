# Contractor Onboarding

## Day one access

- Email account on contractor.example.com domain.
- Slack invite, scoped to the project channels and `#contractors`.
- 1Password vault access scoped to the project's shared items only.

## What contractors get

- Workspace orientation document with how to file invoices, where to upload deliverables, who to escalate to.
- A primary engineering contact.

## What contractors do not get

- Production database access.
- Customer PII unless explicitly scoped in the SOW.
- All-hands meetings (unless invited for a specific topic).

## Offboarding

When the contract ends, revoke email + Slack + 1Password access on the same day. Archive shared deliverables in the project folder. Update the contractor record in Workday.
