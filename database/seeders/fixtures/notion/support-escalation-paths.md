# Support Escalation Paths

## Tier 1 — first response

Frontline support resolves billing questions, account access, and known-good troubleshooting. Target first response under 4 hours, business hours.

## Tier 2 — investigation

Escalation to the technical support team for issues that require log access or reproducing the problem. Target response under 24 hours. Most tickets resolve here.

## Tier 3 — engineering

Tickets escalated to engineering require a confirmed bug or an architectural question. Engineering response is targeted at next business day.

## Out-of-band

Sev-1 incidents go through the security escalation path described in the Drive doc. Sev-2 service degradations go to the on-call engineer.

## What we do not escalate

- "I want a feature." Route to the product team via the customer-feedback intake.
- "I disagree with the pricing." Route to your account exec.
