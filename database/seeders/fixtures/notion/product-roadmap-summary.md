# Product Roadmap Summary

## H1

- Ship the new onboarding flow. Goal: reduce time-to-first-success by 40%.
- Migrate billing from legacy provider to Stripe Billing.
- Beta of the data export feature.

## H2

- General availability of data export.
- Multi-region writes for the API.
- A search experience that competes with the third-party vendor we're paying for today.

## Working agreement

The roadmap is updated monthly. Items above are commitments at a quarter granularity, not week-by-week. Slippage gets discussed in the monthly product review — silently dropping items is not the agreement.
