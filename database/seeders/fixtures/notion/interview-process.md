# Interview Process

## Stages

1. Recruiter screen — 30 minutes. Confirm role fit, compensation expectations, work authorization.
2. Hiring manager call — 45 minutes. Discuss past projects, what the candidate is looking for.
3. Technical loop — 3 to 4 hours, depending on role. System design + coding + behavioural + values.
4. Debrief — within 2 business days. Hire / no-hire decision is unanimous; one no-hire vote means no hire.

## Compensation

Offers are made within 3 business days of the debrief. Candidates have 5 business days to respond.

## Candidate experience

- All candidates receive a debrief from the recruiter, regardless of outcome.
- Candidates can request feedback after the loop; we provide one-call summary, not a written report.
