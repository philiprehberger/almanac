# Expense Reimbursement Policy

## Eligible expenses

- Business travel (flights, lodging, ground transport at booking-comparable rates).
- Meals during business travel up to $75/day.
- Software subscriptions for one-time use cleared by your manager in advance.
- Office supplies under $200/quarter, no approval needed.

## Submission

Submit expense reports in Expensify within 30 days of incurring the expense. Receipts are required for any single item over $25.

## Reimbursement timing

Approved expenses are paid in the next payroll cycle following approval. Manager approval is required for items over $500.

## Non-reimbursable

Personal entertainment, alcohol without a business purpose, fines or penalties, traffic violations, and any item already covered by a stipend.
