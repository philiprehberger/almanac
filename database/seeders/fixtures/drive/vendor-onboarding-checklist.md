# Vendor Onboarding Checklist

## Before signing

- Confirm the vendor passes our security review. Vendors handling customer PII require SOC 2 Type II or equivalent.
- Confirm the contract allows offboarding within 30 days with no penalty for cause.
- Confirm pricing reflects the negotiated rate, not list.

## At sign

- Add the vendor to the contracts vault.
- Create a vendor record in NetSuite with billing terms, contract end date, and renewal contact.
- Add the vendor's primary contact to the vendors Slack channel for quick ops questions.

## Ongoing

- Quarterly review with the technical owner: are we using the product? Are we paying for capacity we don't use?
- Annual security re-attestation for vendors handling PII.

## Offboarding

- 30 days before contract end: technical owner reports on whether to renew, switch, or drop.
- At contract end: revoke all access tokens, archive shared data, remove from billing, mark the contract as terminated.
