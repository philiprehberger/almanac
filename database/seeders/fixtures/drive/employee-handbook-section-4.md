# Employee Handbook §4 — Benefits

## §4.1 — Health insurance

Full-time employees are eligible for health insurance starting on the first day of the month following their start date. The company covers 80% of the employee premium and 50% of dependents'.

## §4.2 — Paid time off

Standard PTO begins at 15 days per year for new employees and increases per the PTO Policy 2025. PTO does not count holidays, sick days, or bereavement leave separately tracked.

## §4.3 — Retirement

401(k) matching at 4% with immediate vesting. Enrollment opens at month three of employment. The plan administrator is Vanguard.

## §4.4 — Stipends

- Home office: $750 one-time after probation.
- Wellness: $50/month, no rollover.
- Education: $1,500/year, approval required for courses over $500.

## §4.5 — Holidays

11 paid holidays: New Year's Day, MLK Day, Presidents' Day, Memorial Day, Juneteenth, Independence Day, Labor Day, Thanksgiving + the Friday after, Christmas Eve, Christmas Day.
