# PTO Policy 2025

## Accrual

New employees accrue 15 days of paid time off per year, increasing to 20 days after three years of service and 25 days after five years. Accrual happens on a per-pay-period basis. Time off is granted in hour-level increments.

## Carryover

Unused PTO carries over up to 40 hours into the next calendar year. Any balance above 40 hours is paid out as cash in the first February paycheck of the following year. Carryover is calculated as of December 31.

## Requesting time off

Submit PTO requests in Workday at least two weeks in advance for blocks of three or more days. Same-day requests must be approved by your manager and are reserved for illness, family emergencies, or unforeseen events.

## Sick leave and bereavement

Sick leave is unlimited and runs separately from PTO. Bereavement leave is five paid days for an immediate family member, three days for an extended family member.

## Unpaid leave

Unpaid leave beyond accrued PTO requires manager approval and is reviewed case-by-case. Returning from unpaid leave longer than 30 days requires HR re-onboarding.
