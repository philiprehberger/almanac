# Code of Conduct

## What we expect

Treat colleagues, customers, partners, and the public with respect. Do not harass, demean, or threaten anyone. Disagree about ideas; do not attack people.

## Reporting concerns

Concerns about conduct can be raised with any manager, with HR at hr@example.com, or anonymously via the EthicsPoint hotline. The company does not retaliate against employees who report concerns in good faith.

## Confidentiality

Information shared during a conduct investigation is restricted to those who need to know to investigate or remediate. Investigators do not share details with managers outside the chain involved in the matter.

## Outcomes

Confirmed violations can lead to mandatory training, formal warnings, suspension, or termination, depending on severity and pattern.
